MLOps and Security
Wow oh wow have we got a special meetup for you all this week! One of the original creators of kubeflow is going to sit down with us and talk about how we can and should be doing MLOps to keep our systems safe.

David Aronchick, Head of Open Source ML Strategy @ Azure, will talk to us about How Kubeflow & MLOps Can Help Secure Your ML Workloads. At the end of the meetup we will understand more about the common attack vectors for ML models and how to build systems to detect and defend against them.

Its gearing up to be one to remember especially as very little attention has been paid to the ways that ML security can go wrong when moving from development to production. Check out some of his past talks here and here.
