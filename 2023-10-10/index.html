All About Evaluating LLM Applications // Shahul Es // MLOps Podcast

Some things just make you break in to a sweat.

When there’s 5 minutes left in your exam and you notice the page is actually double-sided.

But worry not!

This week’s podcast with Shahul Es will help settle those heart palpitations as he gets into the crucial role of evaluation in debugging and troubleshooting. He talks through challenges with evaluating models using open data sets, as well as the limitations of current benchmarks.

As well as discussing solutions like finetuning and evaluating in a custom data distribution, he has some interesting insights into the future of evaluation and finetuning.

Introducing Vizro

On September 26th 2023, McKinsey launched a new GitHub organization to host its digital, cloud and AI open-source projects. This milestone follows the open-sourcing of Kedro, including its placement in LF AI & Data, and the acquisition of Iguazio, where open source is part of their strategy with MLRun and Nuclio. The first significant project in this new series is Vizro, a toolkit for creating modular data visualization applications.
Vizro combines benefits of the speed and user-friendliness of low-code visual analytics tools with the flexibility and power of high-code tools - whilst providing inbuilt visual design, application architecture and coding standardization which also facilitates scaling and reusability. You get inbuilt visual styling in minutes and reduce the amount of “glue code” required.

Vizro’s users have reported it as being a game changer, helping them to bring their vision to life more easily, and accelerate their existing processes — such as being able to visualize and discover raw data quality issues ten times faster than before.

Check it out on GitHub and consult the Vizro documentation.
