Past Meetup
Drift is Inevitable
This was our fourth session on ML monitoring and the final did not fail to go out with a bang! Amy from Fiddler did a deep dive into 3 kinds of model drift that you can encounter while models are out in the wild. I loved her description of each of these as they pertain to a loan application model.

  • Concept Drift - An income level that was earlier considered creditworthy is now considered riskier.
  • Label Drift - A larger proportion of credit-worthy applications start showing up.
  • Feature Drift - Incomes of most applicants increase or decrease. Or you suddenly get more applications from one region.

*This was a sponsored meetup. Huge thanks to the Fiddler folks for their continued support of this community.

Keto? Nah, Kedro
In the beginning of 2010s the software industry came up with the 12-Factor App methodology which had a tremendous impact on the way people build software so it can be deployed and operated easily.

That change in culture combined with the later introduction of Docker was a boon for advancing the DevOps maturity of teams around the globe. Ten years later the data applications world is struggling with getting pipelines to production due to problems that can be easily solved by a similar cultural shift.

This talk will show how Kedro can help you build data apps in the spirit of the 12-Factor App methodology and advance the MLOps maturity of your teams.
