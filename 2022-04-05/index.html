Coffee Session
A Journey in Scaling ML
We chatted with Gabriel Straub, Chief Data Officer at Ocado Technology in our latest Coffee Session. And I know we say this a lot, but it was a 'can't miss' session, for sure.

Key Takeaways
- Distinguish between data producers and data consumers. That should impact the creation and management of your data platform.
- Understand how your customers see the value you provide. ML is great but you need to be able to demonstrate the value proposition of it and show consumers how it makes it easier for them.
- Data governance should be less about access control and more about utility of data.
