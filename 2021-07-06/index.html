Security, Security, Security
Understand your risks (or else!)

Just this past week, we saw a major $70M ransomware attack on Kaseya, which crippled thousands of companies. It's almost as if a day doesn't go by without hearing about a cyberattack on a leading MNC. With all these things happening, doesn't it make sense that we have such a focus on cybersecurity in MLOps?

Diego Oppenheimer, the CEO of Algorithmia, joined us to talk more about hsi phenomenon. Diego was incredibly insightful as he described the basic forms of security threats and risks that can occur in the context of MLOps. We discussed what ML Engineers should know as they work on security related workflows and topics, and where they should reach out to DevSecOps professionals for assistance.

My favorite part of the conversation revolved around how Diego described the key insight of security: it's all about awareness of risk. You don't need to adopt the most stringent protocol for every single product or platform to be secure! You simply need to critically analyze what kind of risks you're taking on when you opt for organizational flexibility or more strict security standards. After all, IT can't be a barrier to innovation. Check out Diego's coffee session to get schooled on security (and read this blog post too)!
