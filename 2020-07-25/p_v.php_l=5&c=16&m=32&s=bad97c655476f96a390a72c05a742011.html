Phew! Another big week under our belt and I gotta say this week feels like there have been some seriously informative conversations happening in our MLOps Slack. I think we may have hit a tipping point 😱
 
 
Help, I need Somebody
We are currently meeting behind the scenes to orchestrate a new project. It is centered around creating an opportunity for the community to have hands-on experience to common problems that tend to crop up in the MLOps universe.

If anyone is interested in being part of the organizational team for this please reach out, we are looking for 2-3 engineers that have experience building production ML systems.
Git-based CI/CD for ML and MLOps
There have been a number of questions floating around in slack about CI/CD for ML and what are some best practices. This week we are going to talk to Yaron Haviv to hear his take on things!

More specifically in this meetup you will learn:

  1. What are the main challenges when moving from ML research environments to production pipeline deployments and how to overcome those
  2. How to automatically convert data-science code from notebooks or code to elastic microservices/functions on Kubernetes and Kubeflow
  3. How to leverage Git to automate ML pipelines, accelerate model development, and enable robust CI/CD processes which cut time to deployment
  4. How data-scientists, data-engineers and ml engineers can collaborate effectively to reduce friction and increase productivity using MLOps practices
