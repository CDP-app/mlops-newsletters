MLOps Community Podcast
Ads Ranking Evolution at Pinterest // Aayush Mudgal // MLOps Podcast #211

From evaluation in the last podcast to evolution in this one.

It’s no easy task either, and Aayush was really honest about how they’ve been doing it at Pinterest from 2018 to today.

He talks about the transition from cascading and scalding to Spark jobs due to scalability and flexibility needs, switching from Tensorflow one to Pytorch, and the transition from XGBoost-based GBTDs to deep learning models in recommendation systems. Plus the shift in their optimization strategy and the process of identifying users in a buying state and figuring out which ads to present to them.

Through each of these transitions they’re always thinking about the ROI every time they evolve, or as my dad used to say, “Is the juice worth the squeeze?”
