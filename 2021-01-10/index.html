Who Made This?
Adria Romero is the cocreator of ProductizeML. Romero is also an AI, ML, and product enthusiast with more than 4 years of professional experience on his mission to empower society with data and AI-driven solutions.

Straight From The Horse's Mouth
From the website you will see the whole focus of what Adria and team are trying to do is this:

"Productize ML provides guidelines and best practices for managing the end-to-end Machine Learning life cycle and its productization."

What Is Real Time?
Real-Time Feature Pipelines: One Man's Journey
Hendrik broke down three phases of his ML journey and how they all related to each other in the most recent meetup. I love diving into seeing how each time he "graduated" from one architecture he brought the learnings with him and was able to say "right, I'm never gonna do that one again!"

Real Time Is Hard
I appreciate his honesty and transparency as he laid out the pros and cons of all the different phases he has gone through so far and how sometimes he thought he was moving forward yet later he realized maybe it wasn't the best idea.

+ Hendrik told us stories of having to use the language F# as a go-between of R and C#. Shoutout to anyone that has ever had to use that and knows his pain!
